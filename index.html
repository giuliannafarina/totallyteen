How we got inspired:

We have created a website that was made for teens by teens. The biggest question we ask when hanging out with our friends is where are we going and what are we doing? Then comes the issue of pricing and distance from home. Our group recently became friends from a summer program we did together but we all come from different areas of New York and New Jersey. This site lets us find fun places near all of us that can accomodate for all of us.
